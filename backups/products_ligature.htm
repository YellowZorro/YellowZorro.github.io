The Rohäb Versa - The best ligature of the year

Nowadays, it seems as though the young'uns have had all the fun of the clarinet. Data shows that between the ages of 80-100, the number of clarinet players decreases dramatically, likely due to the instrument not being as fun at such an old age. You, as the elder one, should play better through experience, not just because they bought a fancy clarinet. What we have here is the Rohäb Versa, a revolutionary type of ligature made just for you. Retain your past experience with the Rohäb Versa, which makes your reed softer for those times when you're too tired to play. In your community band, for family and friends, even for yourself, the Rohab Versa is for an occasion that fits everyone.
