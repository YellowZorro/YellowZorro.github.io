The Möbius Clarinets Company - For all of your Clarinet needs.

From the heady heights of the Room 101, having our heads in the sky comes naturally. With lofty goals and large government grants, we can achieve anything.

At our headquarters, we hand-produce state-of-the-art equipment that is suited to players of any playing ability. Whether you are a younger player just starting out, or an older, experienced clarinetist, we will surely have the equipment that you need.

Unlike our competition, who maliciously produces low-grade equipment to hinder your playing ability and drag you back behind your peers, we only produce the highest-quality clarinets and accessories.

So we welcome you to Möbius Clarinets. We hope that you find everything you are looking for, and much more.
