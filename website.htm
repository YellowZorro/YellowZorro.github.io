The Ministry of Love presents: Torture and Interrogation Around the World

It has recently come to our attention here at Miniluv that our citizens do not really know what goes on inside the Ministry. Obviously this is a concern, since the principles of Ingsoc recommend that we are open with our citizens.

As a result, we have compiled this website, to show the citizens of London what goes on inside the Ministry of Love. You will find that those who are sent here enter into an intensive program of rehabilitation which helps them repent their thoughtcrimes and reaffirm their love for Big Brother.

However, you will notice, that citizens in other countries do not have a government caring enough to offer there services. Many criminals in Eurasia and Eastasia are tortured and killed for their crimes, sometimes unjustly. So we hope that you realize how fortunate you are to be under the benevolent eye of Big Brother.

To get started, please visit the Overview page.

Yours truly,

Inner Party Members Zach, David, Balazs, and Rohan.
